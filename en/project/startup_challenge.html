Project overview and background

The month after our return from nine months of professional experience abroad, three weeks of challenge were more than welcome to return to the school benches. Each week focused on one aspect of our profile: Innovator, Entrepreneur and Project Manager. Working as a team, we first had to respond to a problem faced by a company. In our case, 4D Pionners, a company specializing in 3D printing, told us about the difficulties they were experiencing in sourcing supplies and getting to know local players. In the second week, we brought the economic and commercial dimensions to this solution, making sure that the company's problem wasn't specific, but instead was part of a highly profitable and penetrable market. Clearly, the problem can be a business opportunity. Finally, the last week focused on project management. We put forward short/medium/long-term projects, looking for example at manpower, economic forecasts and geographical deployment.

Each project week being independent, we ended the last week of the project by presenting our project to an exceptional jury: a former manager from the french DPA (Defence Procurement Agency), the Managing Director of Réseaux Entreprendre Nord and two of the three founders of gare ta bécane (Subter. group).

The solution we came up with was a referencing and exchange platform for local players in the 3D printing world. The potential for development exists in being able to roll out this solution to other business sectors and regions. This solution offered several advantages :

  • Visualize : The listing of local players enables you to keep up to date with potential new partners and new competitors in the 3D printing market in a specific geographical area.
  • Compare : Users can filter results to distinguish between suppliers, buyers and printers.
  • Get informed : a specific section for each actor is available, offering the user all the contact details for the same actor, along with reviews and comments left by its customers and its specifications.

If I could remember just one thing :

To check results : My team and I had committed ourselves to presenting a functional and useful solution. So we regularly consulted our project management teachers on the planning and project management aspects, and the head of 4D Pionneers to ensure that the direction we were taking made sense to them.
